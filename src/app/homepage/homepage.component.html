Oaxaca Streetchildren Grassroots

Welcome

Oaxaca Streetchildren Grassroots serves children living in poverty in Oaxaca, Mexico who do not have access to an education. We believe in education as a human right and help the poorest of Oaxaca’s children realize their dream of going to school. When you give a child money on the street you feed them for a day - when you give a child an education you feed them for life!
